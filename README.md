"# Ai-Powered-Legal-Assistant" 

## Limitations

While this multi-agent AI framework demonstrates robust capabilities, it is subject to several fundamental limitations that are important to consider for rigorous academic evaluation. First, the overall performance of the system relies heavily on the quality of the prompts provided to the agents; suboptimal prompt design can directly compromise the structural integrity and reasoning of the output. Second, the machine learning predictions may exhibit inherent biases, as the underlying training dataset is synthetically generated rather than derived from organically labeled real-world data. Third, the system operates without an external factual grounding mechanism, such as Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Consequently, the agents may occasionally generate generic or factually unsupported claims. Furthermore, our quantitative evaluation relies on traditional NLP metrics, such as BLEU and ROUGE, which primarily measure lexical overlap rather than the true correctness or logical depth of the reasoning. Finally, due to computational constraints, the framework relies on lightweight, localized language models rather than state-of-the-art, large-scale foundational models, which may inherently limit the depth of its analytical reasoning.

## Real-World Application: Policy Decision Support

This multi-agent AI system provides substantial value in real-world scenarios, particularly in **Policy Decision Support**. When confronted with a complex policy question, stakeholders can input the issue directly into the system. In response, the autonomous agents comprehensively generate structured, multi-dimensional Pro and Con arguments. The system's hybrid judge—combining deterministic rules, machine learning pattern recognition, and an LLM tiebreaker—then rigorously evaluates these arguments to intelligently select the most logically sound outcome.

By automating this dialectical process, the framework actively reduces human cognitive bias and improves overall decision transparency. It provides policymakers and corporate stakeholders with a clear, balanced, and evidence-driven overview of an issue, empowering them to objectively compare perspectives and make highly informed, strategic decisions.
